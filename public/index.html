About Drafta

Drafta is a revolutionary digital platform providing automated talent search and recruitment for companies, and valuable experiential learning for students. Drafta was initiated in mid-2021 by a group of students from the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology, Surabaya who wanted to change the future of the nation's employment. We aim to solve the high unemployment rate for university graduates in Indonesia and become the leading network of Indonesia's future workforce.
